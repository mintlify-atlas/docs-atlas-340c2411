The **Music Performance Anxiety Diagnostic Questionnaire (MPADQ)** is an online assessment tool designed to help musicians identify, measure, and understand their experience of performance anxiety. Built as the official companion to *Take Charge of Your Performance Anxiety: A Personalized Approach to Conquering Stage Fright* by Dr. Heather Nicole Winter, the MPADQ translates clinical research into a personalized, actionable report — connecting your unique anxiety profile directly to the chapters of the book that will help you most.

## What Is the MPADQ?

The MPADQ is a structured diagnostic questionnaire hosted at [performanceanxietyquestionnaire.com](http://performanceanxietyquestionnaire.com). It consists of two sections:

- **15 demographic questions** that capture background information about you as a musician — your instrument, performance experience, training, and more.
- **157 Likert-scale questions** that measure how strongly you agree or disagree with statements across a range of psychological and situational factors tied to performance anxiety.

Together, these questions produce a detailed scoring profile that breaks your anxiety down into sub-categories and broad categories, pointing you toward the specific sections of *Take Charge of Your Performance Anxiety* that are most relevant to your results.

## Its Relationship to *Take Charge of Your Performance Anxiety*

Dr. Heather Nicole Winter developed the MPADQ as an integral part of *Take Charge of Your Performance Anxiety*. Rather than reading the book cover to cover without context, you take the questionnaire first and receive a personalized roadmap. Your scores indicate which contributing factors — physical, cognitive, behavioral, or situational — are most active in your anxiety, so you can prioritize the chapters that directly address your needs. The MPADQ transforms the book from a general resource into a targeted, individualized guide.

## What You Gain by Completing It

Completing the MPADQ gives you:

- **A personalized score profile** across multiple sub-categories and broad categories of performance anxiety.
- **Targeted chapter recommendations** from *Take Charge of Your Performance Anxiety* based on your individual results.
- **Deeper self-awareness** about the psychological, physical, and situational factors that shape your experience of performing.
- **A referenceable record** — once you have completed the quiz, you can re-enter your key at any time to review your results.

## Key Facts at a Glance

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| Total questions | 172 (15 demographic + 157 Likert) |
| Estimated completion time | ~30 minutes |
| Response scale | 1 (Strongly Disagree) – 5 (Strongly Agree), plus N/A |
| Key type | 10-character alphanumeric, one-time use |
| Results | Permanently accessible after completion |

## Confidentiality

Your individual responses are kept private and will not be shared or identified publicly. However, the combined responses of all participants may be used for statistical research regarding performance anxiety prevalence, symptomatology, and related factors. By completing the questionnaire, you contribute to a growing body of research that benefits the broader community of musicians.
